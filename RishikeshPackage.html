Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Rishikesh

Arrive in Rishikesh, check into your hotel, and witness the soul-stirring Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat in the evening.

Day 2: Spiritual Exploration

Explore Laxman Jhula, Ram Jhula, and Bharat Mandir. Join a morning yoga session by the Ganges for a rejuvenating start.

Day 3: Adventure Day

Experience the thrill of white-water rafting on the Ganges (Grade III/IV rapids). Optional bungee jumping for adrenaline junkies.

Day 4: Nature & Meditation

Trek to the serene Neer Garh Waterfall and participate in a guided meditation session at a peaceful ashram.

Day 5: Departure

Enjoy a free morning for shopping at local markets or relaxing by the river. Depart with cherished memories.

Travel Tips

  • Best Time to Visit: March to May and September to November for pleasant weather.
  • What to Pack: Comfortable clothing, sturdy shoes for trekking, swimwear for rafting, and a light jacket for evenings.
  • Health Precautions: Carry sunscreen, mosquito repellent, and stay hydrated during outdoor activities.
  • Local Etiquette: Dress modestly at religious sites and remove shoes before entering temples.
  • Currency: Carry cash for local markets, as some vendors may not accept cards.
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi is available in hotels, but mobile signals may be weak in remote areas.
