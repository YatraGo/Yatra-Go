About Nainital Lake

Nainital Lake, fondly called Naini Lake, is a pristine freshwater lake nestled in the heart of Nainital, Uttarakhand. Surrounded by seven lush hills, this crescent-shaped lake is the crown jewel of the Kumaon region, drawing travelers with its breathtaking beauty.

Steeped in mythology, the lake is considered one of the 64 Shakti Peeths, where the eyes (Naina) of Goddess Sati are believed to have fallen. Its tranquil waters, framed by verdant hills and colonial-era architecture, create a perfect blend of nature and culture.

Whether you're boating, strolling along the lake, or simply soaking in the views, Nainital Lake promises an unforgettable experience for every visitor.
